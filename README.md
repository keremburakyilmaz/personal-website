# Welcome

This repository is the home of **[keremburakyilmaz.com](https://keremburakyilmaz.com)** - my personal site. If you are here from GitHub, the live version is what you are meant to explore; this page is a short map of what you will find there.

## What is on the site

**Home** introduces who I am and how I like to work-systems, ML, and full-stack products.

**Projects** is a curated list of work I am proud of: listening-pattern ML, fine-tuning experiments, privacy tooling, data warehouses, MLOps, and more. Each entry summarizes what I built and why it mattered.

**Resume** is a readable timeline of roles, studies, and the kinds of problems I have shipped against in the real world.

**Contact** is the place to say hello. You will find email and phone there, and a simple form if you prefer to write in the browser.

**Spotify Brain** is a live dashboard tied to a personal listening project-charts and signals from an ML pipeline I run on my own data.

**Market Radar** is a demo-style view of market and macro charts-useful as a sample of how I present dense time-series data on the web.

**Palimpsest** is an interactive story you play in the browser. It is meant to be read slowly; your choices are remembered for your next visit on the same device.
